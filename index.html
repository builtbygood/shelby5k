About

Shelby Neese

In 1990, after serving on the foreign mission field, Shelby and Mary Neese packed their family and moved to the Flowood, Mississippi area to take a position on staff at Pinelake Church. During his long tenure at Pinelake, Shelby served as an incredible choir director and music minister, touching the lives of thousands in our community through his love of worship and people.


Because Shelby and Mary maintained a passion in their hearts for missions, in 2009, they migrated into full-time international mission work for a second season. They have not only served our community and the greater Jackson metro area, they have also served the nations with their whole hearts, surrendering their very lives to the great cause of loving the world.


On January 26th, while living at home in Mississippi to be close to his children, Shelby was involved in an accident. This accident has left him in need of great medical attention. We are all praying for he and his family. A Facebook page, "Praying for Shelby Neese Family" is growing daily as people rally around such a wonderful family. We invite you to join this Facebook page for updates and we invite you to consider donating time and resources to host a 5K event and one mile fun run for Shelby and family on April 16th at the Flowood Nature Trail.
